Gluten-Free, Sugar-Free Pumpkin Cookies

  • Servings: 24 cookies
  • Prep time: 15-20 min
  • Cook time: 15-20 min
  • Total time: 30-40 min

Ingredients:

3 cups brown rice flour 1 cup arrowroot powder/starch 1 cup tapioca flour 1 tsp psyllium husk powder 1 tsp baking soda 2 tsp baking powder 1/4 tsp salt 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice 2/3 cup honey 2 cups pumpkin puree* 1/4 cup coconut oil, coconut cream, or applesauce 1/4 cup molasses 3/4 cup chocolate chips, rasins, etc. (optional)

Instructions:
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Mix all wet ingredients together until well combined.
  3. In a separate bowl, miz all dry ingredients except for the chocolate chips/rasins.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients (slowly) and mix in a little at a time until all combined.
  5. Add chocolate chips/rasins (if desired) and mix again. Cookie dough should be about the consistency of very thick pancake batter.
  6. Prepare one full-sized, two half-sized, or three quarter-sized cookie sheets with your choice of non-stick preparation (grease, cooking spray, parchment paper, baking mat, other lubricant**, etc.).
  7. Spoon batter into 24 cookies and bake for 15-17 minutes or until the center of the cookie rises back up when pushed down.
  8. Let cool and enjoy!
Notes:

* To make pumpkin puree at home, use pumpkin boiled or roasted until soft and then puree. If you use canned puree, just note that the color of your cookies may come out different.

** Just so long as you don't use WD-40!
